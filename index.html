Bio

I began my game design journey after receiving inspiration from Destiny created by Bungie and my best friend Josh who saw a future game developer in me. At first I felt it was just an idea and didn't think much of it but then I started to have a desire to envision games that were unique and provided a personalized experience for players such as myself. After some research I made a decision to pursue game design. Unfortunately, there has been several roadblocks and situations that have occurred along my journey preventing me from becoming a professional, however, I believe in my pursuit and will not give up on becoming a game designer. In fact, I seek to become a Narrative Designer or Creative Director. I desire to see the worlds I envision come to life before my eyes.

Strong passion for video games. Easily adaptable to environment. Driven by personal growth, values, passions, and self-discovery.

How did I become the General, 2nd to the leader of Espers? It all started when a gaming friend introduced me to Final Fantasy XIV Online back in 2020. At first I began as a solo player and was very invested in the game. After a month of playing and completing the current story at the time, I started to hang around the central market. To my surprise a stranger approached me and sent a message saying, "OMG! I love your outfit!" I immediately had the impression I was speaking to a gamer girl, her name was Momo, so I told her I would help her get the gear if she wanted it. Afterwards she and I exchanged friend requests and then farmed the activity for a short time. Then a week goes by and I see Momo recruiting for her free company, so I asked about joining and she sends me an invite to her free company. After joining Espers of Kalm I began meeting new people and helping out around the free company, such as creating events for farming items or completing activities. Later on, I come to a point where I've been invited to become a community moderator in the free company and on Discord, which I accepted of course. At this point I've been playing FFXIV for about 6-8 months and have been a very reliable member of the free company, as well as, I had an in-game wedding ceremony to marry Momo. Thus, I became the General of Espers of Kalm, 2nd to Momo the True Queen. One very special thing I'd like to mention is that I had the pleasure and honor of meeting Momo in-person after 2 years of friendship by attending her real life wedding. I traveled from the East Coast to the West Coast. It was one of those opportunities you just couldn't miss.
