Choose a path of your own volition, disregard the whispers and screams.
The light shall be bestowed upon your soul, piercing the heavens, dispelling the darkened voices and ghostly figures around you.

Come child, fly through the heavens, pierce the clouds with your magnificence, let your wings guide you on the winds toward thine own skyward castle.

Sit amongst the stars and trees, listen to the falling rain, observe the beauty of your surroundings, keeping an opened mind, you may find the treasure you seek.

Thou shall not infringe upon thine own feet, if one does not desire despair or suffering.

In the cityscape listening to the ambient music of the minstrel’s ballad, you will find inspirational beauty amongst the suffrages of the living.

You must live in the present, else you will seek the past. Present opportunities will be your tool to carving a path toward the future.

Be a wandering soul, not a soulless husk.

Wander around in the darkness and search for an escape route, instead of sitting in the corner pondering the hypotheticals and the unknowns.

The strings of the minstrel’s lute resonate with the surrounding souls, the thrumming sounds will guide them to their destination.

Inspire others so that others may inspire you.

Be not afraid to wander alone in the darkness, the darkness and the unknown can be a comforting place, although do not stay too long else the demons will come, taking you to the deepest depths of despair.

Thou shall not disturb one who is made of stone, else you will incur their silently unpredictable calm wrath.

Paths diverge, pillars will rise and fall, keep moving, envision your destination, don’t stray too long in places you don’t belong, have faith in your pursuit, create objectives not goals, goals have an end, your path will never end, you must have objectives that can be achieved at anytime or moment during your journey.
